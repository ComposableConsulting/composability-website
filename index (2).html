About

Built for deep tech that needs to ship

Composable Consulting exists to solve one specific problem: technically brilliant companies that struggle to build the commercial infrastructure to grow. We close that gap.

Composable Consulting is a boutique technology consulting practice specializing in go-to-market strategy, enterprise sales, channel development, and product marketing for deep technology companies. The practice was built around a specific observation: the hardest technology to sell is often the most important, and the people who build it rarely have the commercial infrastructure to take it to market at scale.

The practice operates in verticals where technical complexity is a feature, not a bug: GPU networking, spatial audio, data center infrastructure, telco edge, semiconductor technology, and AI-powered software. These are markets where being fluent in the technology is a prerequisite for building credibility with buyers, partners, and channel programs.

What makes this practice different is the combination of 20+ years of enterprise technology sales experience, hands-on technical exposure at the hardware and software level, and a curated network of subject matter experts that can be brought to bear on any engagement. When a deal requires a GPU architect, a DSP specialist, or a telco infrastructure expert, one is already accessible. That bench is a structural advantage in complex enterprise sales cycles.

The practice is deliberately small. A limited number of active engagements ensures each client gets the depth of attention that complex, early-stage GTM requires. This is not a high-volume advisory practice. It is an embedded commercial partnership.

Current engagements include serving as Head of Sales at Juice Labs, the inventors of GPU-over-IP technology, and as Sales and Marketing Advisor to BACCH Labs, a breakthrough spatial audio technology company preparing for a major market launch. Both reflect the practice's core focus: technically complex, early-stage companies with transformative technology that needs a commercial engine built around it.

Composable Consulting is based in San Clemente, California, with active client relationships across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.
